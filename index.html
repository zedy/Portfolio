Web development

Everything starts as an idea, and the foundation which ensures the idea is realized as a great website, is planning.
With the first and most crucial step made, we move on to the second which is the injection of carefully mixed balance of skill, experience, with a variety of tools (as listed above), to help us achieve the final goal of clean and price coding, a good user experience and most importantly client satisfaction.

Responsive websites

In today's modern and high tech world, where a good presentation can make or break a deal and where we have everything on the go, Your website must fit in. Having a visually captivating website just isn't enough, it's functionality has to be precise and adaptive, that is the key.That said it has to be cross browser friendly, multi platform and cross device capable.
Weather we're making a mobile or desktop first website, ensuring it's responsive and adaptive is one of my top priorities.

Drupal development

One of the best CMS (content management systems) and my personal favorite, if your goal is to have large website full of various and dynamic content then look no further. With Drupal you get governance, content focus, ease of use, security, global changes, dynamic content, multiple editors, large user base, and many many more.
We work on servicing existing templates, creating new ones, developing and maintaining modules, working with views and other contrib. modules, handling content creation and integration, and more.

PSD to HTML

As one of the most fastest growing services this needs no explanation.
If you require a fast, reliable, efficient conversion from a PSD template to a structurally sound, fully semantic, SEO friendly and cross-browser compatible website that meets W3C standards, contact us.

Ajax

We work closely with ajax, it's one of the favorite tools of a web developer, myself included. We consider ajax as the ninja of the web development world, it works hard under the covers of the browser, unseen, to fetch any data the user requires without any of the hassle of going to another page.
If the goal of the website is to be sleek and streamlined, where lots of content is loaded per user request, then ajax is the way to do it.

Web services

Integrating your website with other online web services is very useful, and for some website crucial. We can build our own services or use existing ones, for the purpose of giving a rich client experience.
If your desire is to use public social networking services to collect user data, or to develop your own for whatever purpose, we will work hard to unsure the best possible user experience.

Social Media Integration

Integrating social media into your website is extremely crucial, as it allows customers easier access to your business, if they cannot find you online (due to the lack of information) they will go to your competition.
Thus it is important to promote your `brand` online.

Web site maintanace

Any mechanism with moving parts requires a decent service every now and then, and a website/service is no exception, keeping your machine well oiled and running is as important as it's planning and development phase. Especially if it's a larger project with many interchangeable parts and dynamic content.
Weather You are looking for fixing a bug in the code, adding new content, creating new features, removing old and unused sections we are here to help.
